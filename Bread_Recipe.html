Joel's Bread Recipe

As instructed by Bart Pemberton

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 pkg yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 pinches kosher salt (optional)

Process

Add one teaspoon of sugar and package of yeast to warm water and let sit for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

In medium mixing bowl combine flour, salt and remainder of sugar.

Add water to flour mixture and stir until it forms a ball. Take out of the bowl and knead for about 1 minute.

Return to mixing bowl and allow to rise for 1-2 hours. Dough will double or triple in size.

Preheat oven to 405 F.

Take out of bowl and knead again for a minute or two. Cut dough ball into 3 equal parts. Fold equal parts into balls for about a minute each and place on greased baking sheet. Allow to rise for 10 minutes.

Gently score the tops of your loafs 5-6 times to allow for expansion while baking. Baste the top of the loaf with oil and sprinkle a pinch of kosher salt on the top of the loaf.

Bake for 21 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes. Slice and enjoy.

Variations

Whole Wheat - Substitute 2 cups of whole wheat flour for 2 cups of white flour. Follow directions as usual.

Rye - Substitute 2 cups of rye flour for 2 cups of white flour. Follow directions as usual.

Garlic, Rosemary, Basil or Chive - Add in spices as you see fit.

Pumpkin Seeds, Sunflower Seeds, Flax Seed and Caraway Seed can be added as well.

Other variations include Cinnamon Raison, Cranberry Walnut, Asiago Cheese...
